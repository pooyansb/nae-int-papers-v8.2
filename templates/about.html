About This Application

Photon-counting CT represents a major advancement in detector technology, enabling intrinsic spectral imaging, smaller detector pixels, and improved contrast and dose efficiency. This application offers an interactive platform to explore the impact of these innovations across clinical imaging tasks.

Our database features a curated selection of high-impact PCCT studies (mean IF:8.5) in our upcoming Radiology review article, covering six imaging domains: cardiac, thoracic, neuro, abdominal-oncology, musculoskeletal, and pediatrics.

An integrated Sankey diagram visualizes the connections from PCCT technical features (e.g., ultrahigh spatial resolution, spectral capability) to diagnostic tasks and clinical outcomes. Clicking any stream reveals the set of publications associated with that connection, enabling exploration by feature, task, or outcome.

Note: This is for research use only and is not intended for clinical use. Despite careful implementation of RAG techniques, users are encouraged to validate results directly from the cited publications.
